What is a Software Developer?

Software developers design, build, test, and maintain apps, games, websites, and programs using languages like Python, Java, or HTML.

They write code, fix bugs, create applications, update software, and work alone or in teams.

Where They Work

Developers work in tech companies like Google, Meta, OpenAI, game studios, banks, hospitals, or as freelancers from home.

Skills & Traits

You need creativity, logical thinking, fast problem-solving, tech passion, patience, and teamwork skills.

Important skills: coding knowledge, computer skills, willingness to learn, and teamwork.

Subjects to Study

Core subjects: Pure Mathematics, Information Technology, Computer Applications Technology. Optional: Physical Sciences, Engineering Graphics and Design.

Where to Study

Universities: UCT, Wits, University of Pretoria. Colleges/Bootcamps: UNISA, WeThinkCode_, HyperionDev.

Degree: 3–4 years. Diploma/Bootcamp: 1–2 years.

Salary

Junior: R15,000–R25,000 | Mid-level: R30,000–R60,000 | Senior: R70,000+

Why it’s a Great Career

High demand, work from anywhere, creative, high-paying, and tech is the future!
